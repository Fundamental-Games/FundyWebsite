About

Founded in 2013 by three industry veterans with over 50 years of collective experience and a combined catalogue of over 25 published titles.

Fundamental Games is a multi-discipline, scalable studio, offering our highly-experienced technical and artistic talent as an outsourcing service or as a complete development team.

We provide remote or in-house development that encompasses years of experience, a dedicated work ethic and a firm grasp of what it takes to deliver high-quality games on time and on budget.

Services

Fundamental Games is a remote co-development and outsourcing studio.

We specialise in network/multiplayer and gameplay coding. We are a multi-discipline crew of veterans that can scale to your projects needs.

We've worked with studios large and small, working in tandem with dev teams at Mirosoft, Bethesda, nDreams, Bossa Studios, Silent Games and many more.

We offer the following services:

  • Multiplayer/Network Support (back-end, front-end, matchmaking)
  • Commercial Game Engines (Unreal, Unity, CryEngine)
  • Bespoke / In-House Game Engines
  • Seamless integration into established development teams
  • Flexible support - scaling teams up and down as needed
  • PC, Console, Mobile and VR development
  • Console Ports (Playstation, Xbox, Switch)
